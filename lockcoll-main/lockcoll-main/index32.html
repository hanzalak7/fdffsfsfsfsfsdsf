Welcome To Lokmanya College

Lokmanya Shikshan Prasarak Mandal ASC college Chondhi-Kihim At Chondhi Post Kihim Tal Dist is a private un-aided affiliated college in Alibag, Raigad in the state Maharashtra. Lokmanya Shikshan Mandals Senior College At Chondhi Post Kihim Tal Dist was established in the year 2009 and got affiliated to University Of Mumbai in year 2009. The college is co-educational by its nature. The college has a sum of 1.6 acres of land and the college premises is build within 303 square meters.

Mission

"Provide each and every student quality education, platform to express and exchange freely the views, training them to respect others, building consent. Plan and execute projects for their strong physical and mental health. Prepare them to serve the nation and humanity ensuring their self-development. "

Vision

"To inculcate human values and make them perfect members of civil society and the entire universe in a scientific manner. "

About College

With the thought that economic condition should not be a hurdle to any student who intends to take education at any level, the mass leader ex-MLA Late Madhukarsheth Thakur decided to lead Lokmany Shikshan Prasarak Mandal, the famous educaltional institute in Chondhi area in Alibag tehsil. He was the man of principles and never made business calculations in the field of education. His was the chief aim, “ Wards of poorest of poors must take education.” This was the decade of struggle in sociopolitical and economic struggle of India. The masses generally do not approach leaders easily but Madhukar Thakur could be accessed easily by common man. Honourable Madhukar Thakur respected dignty of person and this assurance made him beloved mass leader. Quality education with affordable cost was his honest motive and always resisted commercialization whatsovever of the education.

LSPM Senior college works according to the principles laid by the Management constituted according to broad vision of the founder, Hon’ble Late Madhukarheth Thakur. After him the LSPM is being lead by Hon’ble Adv. Pravin Madhukar Thakur with his innovative ideas and expertise supported by his strong network of educational resources.

At present there are three faculties Arts, Science and Commerce offering degree level education. There is planning to introduce selected Post-graduate courses also. Entire educational complex, LSMP believes that education must be utility oriented. Thus industry-academic interaction is always encouraged by the Management members. They take every effort to link student community to the industries in the Raigad district, Navi Mumbai, Mumbai and even Pune areas. Many alumni work in the companies and establishments in these areas. The guidelines from University of Mumbai and programmes prescribed by them are always followed in letter and spirit. The Government of Maharashtra and Government of India through there appropriate agencies and institutions support the initiatives by LSPM be they education related or environment and health related.
